The missing link between discovery and library. Detects videos, finds matches, and downloads audio with zero friction.

Add to Chrome How it works

Instant Sync

One-click right-click menu to find and add current videos to your Spotify playlists instantly.

Lossless Downloads

Prioritizes FLAC from Lucida (Qobuz/Tidal) or falls back to high-quality Opus.

Smart Matching

Fuzzy matching algorithms handle "Official Video", "Lyrics", and "Feat." noise automatically.

Privacy First

Runs entirely in your browser. No backend servers, no data collection, no tracking.

Workflow

1

Install & Connect

Load the extension and connect your Spotify account. The token is stored locally in your browser.

2

Right-Click to Sync

On any YouTube video, right-click and select "Add to Spotify". TunePort finds the track and adds it to your default playlist.

3

Dual Pipeline

If enabled, TunePort simultaneously downloads the highest quality audio file (FLAC/Opus) to your machine.
